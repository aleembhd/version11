Dial Pad User Guide

Real-Time Call Management for Real Estate Agencies

Introduction

The dial pad is a crucial component of our call management system, allowing you to make calls and record their outcomes efficiently. This guide will walk you through using the dial pad feature.

Using the Dial Pad

  1. Accessing the Dial Pad
    • Open the app and navigate to the dial pad screen.
    • You'll see a numeric keypad with digits 0-9, *, and #.
  2. Entering a Phone Number
    • Use the numeric keypad to enter the desired phone number.
    • The entered number will appear in the display area above the keypad.
    • If you make a mistake, use the 'x' button (bottom left) to delete the last digit.
  3. Making a Call
    • Once you've entered the complete number, press the 'Call' button at the bottom of the screen.
  4. 10-Digit Number Requirement
    • The dial pad only works when a perfect 10-digit mobile number is entered.
    • If any digit is missing or if you enter more than 10 digits, an error message will be displayed.
    • Double-check your entry before making the call to avoid errors.
  5. Call Answered Prompt
    • After the call ends, a popup will appear asking, "Was The Call Answered?"
    • Select 'YES' if the call was answered, or 'NO' if it wasn't.
  6. Call Status (If Answered)
    • If you selected 'YES', another popup will appear to record the call status.
    • Choose from three options:
      • Not Interested
      • Interested
      • Callback

Tips

  • Always confirm the number before making the call to avoid errors.
  • Be prepared to quickly categorize the call outcome for accurate record-keeping.
  • If you receive an error about entering a proper 10-digit number, double-check your entry and try again.

Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using the dial pad, please use the help option within the app to contact our development team.
